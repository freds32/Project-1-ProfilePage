I'm Fred aka Slings.

San Diego, California.

Background

As an aspiring web designer navigating my way into the tech world, I bring a diverse background shaped by living in various locations and holding a range of professional roles. Notably, I've spent the last eight years as a professional baseball player, where I honed my discipline and perseverance.

Driven by a passion for creating visually stunning websites, I am eager to infuse my life experiences into the art of web design. I am a dedicated, self-disciplined individual committed to lifelong learning, constantly seeking to expand my skills and deepen my knowledge base.

Goals

I want to master the process of building web sites and increase my knowledge, skills and abilities in:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • Ruby
  • Python

My goal is to leverage my diverse background and passion for creating visually compelling websites to become a skilled web developer. By acquiring expertise in front-end and back-end technologies, mastering UI/UX principles, and continuously expanding my knowledge in emerging trends like responsive design and accessibility, I aim to contribute to innovative web projects that positively impact users' experiences online. Through dedication to lifelong learning and hands-on experience, I aspire to establish myself as a proficient web developer known for delivering high-quality, user-centric solutions.
